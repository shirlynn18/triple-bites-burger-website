Meet The Founders

Lim Ximun - Founder

Ximun is the creative force behind TRIPLE BITES. As someone who grew up loving street food, night markets and quick snacks full of flavor, she dreamed of opening a place where people could find amazing, delicious meals without the long wait. With a deep love for food, casual dining and quality ingredients, Ximun started TRIPLE BITES to shake up the fast food scene — proving that "quick" and "tasty" can go hand-in-hand.

Tan Zhi En - Co-Founder

Zhi En is the heart of operations at TRIPLE BITES. With a background in hospitality and customer experience, she makes every guest feel at home. Her passion lies in creating warm and welcoming dining atmospheres. She believes in fast service, friendly faces and clean inviting spaces. Her goal at TRIPLE BITES is to make sure every guest leaves satisfied and happy, whether they're dining in or grabbing a bite on the go.

Shirlynn Chung Kai Qing - Chairperson

As the Chairperson of TRIPLE BITES, Shirlynn brings both leadership and heart to the table. With extensive experience in business management and brand building, she ensures that TRIPLE BITES continues to grow and thrive. Her leadership style inspires her team to care about their work, their people and their customers in building a brand that champions quality, trust and community.

Our Story

Our Journey

TRIPLE BITES began with a simple question: why settle for fast food that's bland and forgettable? Our founders wanted to offer something different — a place where speed meets flavor, where every bite delivers real satisfaction.

From the very beginning, our mission has been simple: to reimagine fast meals with bold flavor. No frozen patties, no soggy buns — just real food, made with passion for people on the move. Whether you're craving crispy fried wings, juicy burgers or signature sauces you won't find anywhere else, we've got you.

Our mission stays the same: Good food. Fast. With flavor that sticks with you.

Brand Philosophy

Our Core Values

At TRIPLE BITES, we believe fast food should never feel like a compromise. Our brand is built on three core principles:

Freshness – We prepare ingredients daily to ensure every bite bursts with flavor.
Speed – We serve quickly without cutting corners.
Flavor – Our recipes are bold, satisfying and made to stand out.

We want every meal to feel like a little celebration — affordable, fast, but full of taste and care. Whether you're a student on the go, a family on a weekend outing, or someone grabbing a late-night snack, TRIPLE BITES is here to serve you something special.

Meet Our Chef Team

Culinary Experts

Behind the counter, our kitchen team works like a well-oiled machine. But it's not just about speed — it's about heart. Every team member is trained to maintain consistency, safety and presentation, even during the busiest rush.

Our chefs come from different culinary backgrounds, from street food stalls to modern kitchens and they all share one thing: a passion for bold flavors and satisfying meals. Whether they're crafting our signature sauces or perfecting the crisp on our fries, they're focused on delivering food that exceeds expectations.

Ingredients & Food Safety

Quality & Safety

We believe you deserve to know what's going into your food and we take that responsibility seriously. That's why we partner with trusted suppliers to bring in fresh vegetables, quality meat and ingredients that meet safety standards.

Our kitchen follows strict hygiene practices, including regular sanitization, food temperature checks and proper handling procedures. Every team member is trained in food safety protocols because your health and trust matter to us.

From prepare to plate, we prioritize quality, cleanliness and care — so you can enjoy your food worry-free every time.
